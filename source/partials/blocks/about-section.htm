About The IKC

The International Internet Kunio Conference on Computer Science 2021 (IKC'21, IIKCCS'21) is the leading kunio's symposium on Computer Science, bringing together academics, industrial researchers, and practitioners to exchange new ideas, problems, and experience on how to be a kunio.

IKC 2021 will be held in Shanghai, on Oct 2-5, 2021.
